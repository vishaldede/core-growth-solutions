Our story

Founded in 2022, Core Growth Solutions was born from a simple observation: local businesses were being left behind in the AI revolution. Big companies had data scientists and expensive tools; Main Street had guesswork.

We set out to change that. Our team of AI engineers and local market experts built four integrated intelligence engines that give small businesses the same predictive power as Fortune 500 companies — at a fraction of the cost.

Today, we serve over 200 clients across 8 states, from restaurants and retail shops to home services and healthcare providers. And we're just getting started.
